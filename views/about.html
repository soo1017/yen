HAND OUT TO HOLD THEIR HAND

YEN stands for Youth Empowering Nation

Non-Profit Organization

Few youth members started YEN in order to reach out to those kids in need in developing countries short after their mission trip to Haiti. Aiding the kids in the mission field, and helping them out of poverty, lack of education, and lack of guidance to future paths are the motivation and inspiration for the creation of YEN. Our youth is the main core of YEN's drive.

Each YEN chapter in the school is connected to build up the connection with other youth and communicate with other thoughts.

Our youth is inspired to expand our horizon and dreaming big beyond our community and even nation.

YEN benefits both the youth and the children. Youth benefit as the donor to help a good cause, while children are the recipients of the good cause. It will help both parties to grow as a strong influence on their community and exemplify a good citizen.

YEN is also supported by adult members voluntarily. Adult members help YEN by building up its system and managing its regular-based operations to make YEN a smooth-sailing yet effective.

YEN Main Operations


  • Recruiting, taking, and processing donations
  • Connecting and matching a donor and a recipient kid in a mission location as 1:1
  • Selecting and managing mission locations and operations
  • Monitoring and distributing if donations flow into right and designated places
  • ...
